Located in the heart of the Mwenezi District, Nikita Mangena High School is a center of learning named in honor of the liberation hero, General Alfred Nikita Mangena.

We are committed to upholding the values of discipline, hard work, and service that our namesake stood for. Our mission is to provide quality education to the rural community, empowering students to break barriers and achieve their full potential.

From our classrooms to our sports fields, we strive to create an environment where every student is respected and encouraged to excel.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution in Masvingo Province, nurturing disciplined and academically competent citizens ready to serve Zimbabwe.
